**** Managing Director for the Security and Software Engineering Research
Center ****
This posting will expire on December 31, 2013.
Organization/Institution: Ball State University
Department: Computer Science
Posted: October 18, 2013
Contract professional position available immediately.   Responsibilities: 
design and execute a research project management model to assess project
evaluations, identify and improve tech transfer, and estimate ROI throughout
the research process; identify, cultivate, and manage growth opportunities for
the Center.  Minimum qualifications:  master&rsquo;s degree in computer science
or closely related technology field; three years in industry/academia in
software engineering and/or security work; excellent &ldquo;people
skills;&rdquo; good oral and written communication skills; strong
organizational skills; ability to travel; possess and maintain a valid
driver&rsquo;s license and be insurable under the university&rsquo;s auto fleet
policy.  Preferred qualifications:  master&rsquo;s degree in computer science;
managerial experience; strong analytical ability.  Candidates for contract
searches must have current authorization to be employed in the U.S. without
employer sponsorship.
*** Application Instructions ***
Send cover letter, resume, transcript, and names and contact information for
three references to:  Wayne Zage, Director of Security and Software Engineering
Research Center, Department of Computer Science, Ball State University, Muncie,
IN  47306.  Review of applications will begin immediately and will continue
until the position is filled.  (www.bsu.edu)  

Ball State University is an equal opportunity, affirmative action employer and
is strongly and actively committed to diversity within its community.
