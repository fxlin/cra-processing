**** College of Engineering: Professor (Open Rank) – Computer Science ****
This posting will expire on December 31, 2013.
Organization/Institution: University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign
Posted: October 17, 2013
The Department of Computer Science (CS) at the University of Illinois at
Urbana-Champaign invites applications for faculty positions at all levels and
in all areas of CS, but with particular emphasis in the areas of Machine
Learning (ML), its associated theoretical foundations and experimental
applications;  and Big Data and related platforms, systems, and foundational
theory.

Applications are encouraged from candidates whose research programs are in
traditional as well as in nontraditional and interdisciplinary areas of
computer science. The department is engaged in exciting new and expanding
programs for research, education, and professional development, with strong
ties to industry.  Applications are being accepted now.
*** Application Instructions ***
For more information and to apply, please visit:  

cs.illinois.edu/faculty-candidates  

In order to ensure full consideration, applications must be received by
February 15, 2014.

Illinois is an Affirmative Action/Equal Opportunity Employer
(www.inclusiveillinois.illinois.edu) and committed to a family-friendly
environment (http://provost.illinois.edu/worklife/index.html).
