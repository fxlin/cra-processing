**** Assistant Professor ****
This posting will expire on December 31, 2013.
Organization/Institution: Ball State University
Department: Computer Science
Posted: October 29, 2013
Tenure-track faculty position available August 15, 2014.  The primary need is
for instruction in systems courses such as databases and computer architecture,
including both undergraduate and graduate courses with a secondary need in
security and introductory programming sequence as well as developing curricular
modules for parallel computing and database usage to be incorporated into
various courses. Responsibilities: teaching courses for computer science
majors; supervising graduate students; scholarship including publications,
presentations, and/or applying for external funding.  Minimum qualification: 
earned doctorate in computer science or closely related field by August 15,
2014.  Preferred qualifications:  Ph.D. in computer science; demonstrated
teaching ability; record of peer-reviewed publications and/or presentations, or
evidence of other scholarly contributions related to computer systems.
*** Application Instructions ***
Send letter of application, curriculum vitae, official transcripts, and the
names and contact information for three references to:  Dr. Paul Buis,
Chairperson, Department of Computer Science, Ball State University, Muncie, IN 
47306.  Review of applications will begin immediately and will continue until
the position is filled.  (www.bsu.edu)

Ball State University is an equal opportunity, affirmative action employer and
is strongly and actively committed to diversity within its community.
