**** Tenure-Track Assistant Professor ****
This posting will expire on December 31, 2013.
Organization/Institution: The University of Texas at Tyler, TX
Posted: October 25, 2013
Start date: Spring or Fall 2014.

Duties and responsibilities: The successful candidate will establish a strong,
externally funded research program in areas to complement existing departmental
strengths in symbolic computation, database systems, data mining,
bioinformatics and network security. All areas of computer science and computer
information systems will be considered; however, candidates with expertise in
areas that complement departmental strengths are especially encouraged to
apply. Participation in departmental, college, and university committees and
outreach programs is expected; collaborative research with other faculty and
institutions is strongly encouraged.

Qualifications: (i) Ph.D. in any area of computer science or computer
information systems. (ii) Demonstrated research creativity and productivity.
(iii) Ability to develop a strong research program. (iv) Evidence of effective
teaching and other communication skills.

 
*** Application Instructions ***
Applications: Please send (as a single PDF file) a) cover letter; b) detailed
curriculum vitae; c) statement of research interests and professional goals, d)
statement of teaching interests and philosophy; e) reprints of 3 relevant
publications; and f) names and contacts of at least three references as an
attachment to Search Committee, Department of Computer Science, The University
of Texas at Tyler at E-mail: cs@uttyler.edu.

Review of applications will begin December 15, 2013 and continue until a
suitable candidate is found.

The University of Texas at Tyler is an Affirmative Action/Equal Opportunity
Employer. Women and minorities are strongly encouraged to apply.
More details about the position and available resources can be found at http://
www.uttyler.edu/faculty/jobs/cecs/index.php
