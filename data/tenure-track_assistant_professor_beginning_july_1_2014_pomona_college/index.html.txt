**** Tenure-track Assistant Professor, beginning July 1, 2014 ****
This posting will expire on December 31, 2013.
Organization/Institution: Pomona College
Department: Computer Science Department
Posted: October 03, 2013
Teaching load: 2/2. Ph.D. required. Pomona College, the founding member of the
Claremont Colleges, is a highly selective liberal arts college attracting a
diverse, national student body.

 
*** Application Instructions ***
Please submit the following application materials online
(academicjobsonline.org/ajo/jobs/3317): cv; graduate transcripts; 3 or more
letters of recommendation, at least one of which evaluates teaching; and three
brief statements--one addressing teaching philosophy, one addressing
scholarship, and one addressing demonstrated ability to mentor a diverse
student body.

Review of applications will begin on December 31, 2013. Further information at
www.cs.pomona.edu/search2014.html or search@cs.pomona.edu.

The Computer Science Department supports equal access to higher education and
values working in a richly diverse environment. The ideal candidate will have
experience working with students from diverse backgrounds and a demonstrated
commitment to improving higher education for underrepresented students.
