**** Assistant Professor -Computer Systems ****
This posting will expire on December 31, 2013.
Organization/Institution: Farmingdale State College
Posted: October 21, 2013
Job Description: The Department of Computer Systems is seeking to fill a tenure
track position at the rank of Assistant Professor of Computer Systems beginning
Fall 2014. Applicants should be proficient at teaching C++/Java object oriented
programming as well as upper level database courses. Requirements: REQUIRED:
Ph.D. in Computer Science, Computer Information Systems, MIS or closely related
field (from an AACSB accredited university) and established research agenda.
ABD with all degree requirements including dissertation defense by August 2014
will also be considered. Knowledge and demonstrated proficiency in teaching
C++/Java object oriented programming. Applicants must also be proficient at
teaching upper level database courses. Experience teaching at the college level
is required. ABD in Computer Science, MIS, CIS or closely related discipline is
required.

•    This is a full-time, 10 month faculty position available.
•    CLOSING DATE FOR RECEIPT OF APPLICATIONS: November 15, 2013
•    Salary $65,000/yr. and may increase commensurate with experience

State University of New York is an Equal Opportunity/Affirmative Action
Employer
