**** Assistant or Associate Professor ****
This posting will expire on December 31, 2013.
Organization/Institution: Montana State University
Department: Department of Computer Science
Posted: October 21, 2013
The Montana State University Computer Science Department is searching for one
or more faculty members at the Assistant or Associate Professor level who can
contribute to the departments research, teaching and service endeavors, while
working with and engaging diverse students and colleagues within and outside
the CS program.  The department prides itself on its innovative curriculum and
its growing research and Ph.D. program. It attracts highly capable students,
including five recent Goldwater scholars and five recent USA Today Academic All
Americans. Montana State University is a Carnegie Foundation RU/VH research
university with an enrollment of approximately 14,500.

Montana State University&rsquo;s flagship campus is located in Bozeman, a rare
small town that encompasses the energy and cultural offerings of an urban area,
while maintaining a friendly, casual ambience. Surrounded by four mountain
ranges, national forests, spectacular ski resorts and blue-ribbon trout
streams, the area offers an active and inspiring lifestyle.
*** Application Instructions ***
The website http://www.montana.edu/jobs/faculty/14-137 has information on
position requirements and application procedures. Since increasing the
diversity of the computer science profession is a strategic priority, women and
underrepresented minorities in computing are particularly encouraged to apply.
