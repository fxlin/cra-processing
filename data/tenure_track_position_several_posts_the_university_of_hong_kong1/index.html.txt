**** Tenure Track Position (several posts) ****
This posting will expire on December 31, 2013.
Organization/Institution: The University of Hong Kong
Department: Department of Computer Science
Posted: October 17, 2013
Applications are invited for Associate Professor / Assistant Professor, full-
time positions tenable from 1 July 2014.  Applicants should have a PhD degree
in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or a related field, with a strong
commitment to research and teaching.  A solid track record in research is
essential.  The positions are open to all research areas in Computer Science,
with preference given to candidates in the area of bioinformatics, computer
networks, machine learning, robotics or software engineering.
*** Application Instructions ***
More information can be found at: http://jobs.hku.hk/jd.php?id=201300993 and
http://www.cs.hku.hk/

Application and enquiries should be sent to recruitment@cs.hku.hk
