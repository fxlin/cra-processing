**** Tenure Track Faculty in Computer Science ****
This posting will expire on December 31, 2013.
Organization/Institution: Colorado State University
Department: Department of Computer Science
Posted: October 15, 2013
Colorado State University is accepting applications for a tenure-track
assistant professor in Computer Science, beginning fall 2014.  Only candidates
in bioinformatics/computational biology will be considered.  This position is
part of a university-wide effort to recruit additional faculty in biosciences
and bioinformatics.  Applications must be received by December 16, 2013.
*** Application Instructions ***
Submit materials at http://cns.natsci.colostate.edu/employment/Compsci/.

CSU is an EO/EA/AA employer. Colorado State University conducts background
checks on the final candidates.
