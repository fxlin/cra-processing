**** Policy Analyst ****
This posting will expire on December 31, 2013.
Organization/Institution: Computing Research Association (CRA)
Department: Government Affairs Staff
Posted: October 14, 2013
The Computing Research Association, the national voice of the computing
research community, seeks a Policy Analyst for its Government Affairs staff.
This person will work closely with the Director of Government Affairs tracking
and managing their own portfolio of policy issues, providing research support,
planning events, handling some administrative duties, and helping communicate
with CRA&rsquo;s membership.

The ideal candidate will have a Bachelors degree in information technology,
public policy or a related field; some experience in a policy-oriented
environment; some experience planning workshops or briefings; excellent verbal
and written skills; web-skills; and a demonstrated interest in federal research
policy and computing.
*** Application Instructions ***
Interested candidates should submit a resume with cover letter describing their
qualifications and salary requirements via email to analyst@cra.org
